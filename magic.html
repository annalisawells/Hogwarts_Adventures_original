Gaining Magical Know-how: Picking classes at Hogwarts


At each level until level 10, you earn 3 class points to pick your prefferred class schedule. Each class has its own stackable benefits and bonuses, which can help you bolster skills or proficiency in a certain magic type.

Note: You may not reassign those points once you make your selection at each level.
The different types of magic that we will have access to in this world are:

Charms
Conjuration
Curses
Jinx
Mind
Transfiguration

Each spell that you cast will note its magic type, and your bonuses will aid you when you make a cast check. For example, if you have a +3 bonus in Jinx and a total of 5 points in Spellcraft, when you cast Nox(J), you already have 8 points towards your level 1 caster check of 16. Now you only need to roll an 8 on your d20 and you are good to go!

Now have fun and pick your classes!

    First Year:

  1. Transfiguration: +1 Bonus on Transfiguration Spells
  2. Charms: +1 Bonus on Charm Spells
  3. Potions: +1 Bonus on Brew Potions SKILL, +1 HP
  4. History of Magic: +1 Bonus on Conjuration Spells
  5. Defense Against the Dark Arts: +1 Jinx Spells, -1 to penalty for using a curse
  6. Astronomy: +1 Bonus on Mind Spells
  7. Herbology: +1 Bonus on Spellcraft SKILL, +1 HP
  8. Flying: +1 Bonus on fly SKILL, +1 Will Save Bonus

    Second Year:

  1. Transfiguration
  2. Charms
  3. Potions
  4. History of Magic
  5. Defense Against the Dark Arts
  6. Astronomy
  7. Herbology

    Electives for Third and Fourth Year:

  1. Arithmancy
  2. Muggle Studies
  3. Divination
  4. Study of Ancient Runes
  5. Care of Magical Creatures

    Third and Fourth Year:

  1. Core classes: all 7
  2. Electives: pick 2

    Fifth Year:

  1. O.W.L.s determine your future classes and path toward N.E.W.T advanced classes

    Sixth Year:

    N.E.W.T-level classes and specialized classes such as

  1. Advanced Arithmancy Studies
  2. Alchemy
  3. Apparition

    Seventh Year:

    Continuation of N.E.W.T-level classes and specialized classes such as

  1. Advanced Arithmancy Studies
  2. Alchemy
  3. Apparition

END OF YEAR: N.E.W.T and career placement!!
